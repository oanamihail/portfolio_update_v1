About me

I am a strong advocate for user centred design. I believe that understanding your users and acknowledging their needs and frustrations is the key to improving functionality, ease of use and enjoyment.

My experience has taught me that the most successful projects are those that always keep the end-user in the forefront. It is easy to get attached to initial design solutions and forgo any additional research or testing but it rarely works.

Goals

I believe that now my job is more than just designing eye-candy interfaces, my job is to build bridges between the people who want to make this world better than the people who need it. I do a lot of things as a designer, my main focus is on:

  • UX design
  • UI design
  • Branding
  • Prototyping
